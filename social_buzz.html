User Engagement Analysis

  • Category: Data Analysis
  • Tech Stack: Excel, Tableau

Situation:

The simulation at Accenture revolved around advising a hypothetical social media client, demanding a deep dive into the role of a Data Analyst. This presented an opportunity to apply analytical skills in the context of real-world decision-making.

Task:

A key task involved cleaning, modeling, and analyzing seven datasets to extract insights into content trends. The goal was to uncover valuable information that would guide strategic decisions for the social media client.

  • I had to address questions through the analysis, such as:
    1. How many unique categories exist?
    2. What are the top 5 categories, and what is the total reaction for the top category?
    3. Which month experiences the peak in user engagement?

Action:

To accomplish these tasks, I meticulously navigated through the datasets, employing a combination of data cleaning, modeling, and analysis techniques. The resulting dashboard not only answered the client's specific questions but also provided a visual representation of the data.

  • Key actions included:
    1. Data cleaning and modeling
    2. In-depth analysis to uncover content trends
    3. Visual representation of insights in a dashboard format

Result:

The outcome was a comprehensive dashboard showcasing answers to the client's inquiries and revealing significant insights into user engagement and content trends.
