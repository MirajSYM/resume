Situation:

The Automatidata team engaged in a significant project for the New York City Taxi and Limousine Commission (NYC TLC), focusing on constructing a multiple linear regression model to predict taxi fares. This project aimed to leverage existing data collected over a year, showcasing the application of Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and regression modeling.


Explore the notebook here.

Task:

The primary tasks involved performing thorough Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) to understand the dataset, detect correlations, handle outliers, and create meaningful features. Subsequently, the team delved into model building and evaluation, utilizing resources like StandardScaler and LinearRegression from sklearn.

Action:

EDA Purpose:

  • Utilized box plots to visualize and identify outliers in key columns.
  • Handled outliers through imputation and capping techniques.
  • Applied feature engineering to create additional variables, enhancing dataset insights.

Model Building and Evaluation:

  • Employed StandardScaler for data normalization.
  • Split the dataset into training and testing sets.
  • Instantiated and fit the LinearRegression model.
  • Evaluated the model's performance with metrics such as R^2, MAE, MSE, and RMSE.

Interpreting Model Results:

  • Identified mean_distance as a key feature impacting fare amounts.
  • Visualized model accuracy through a scatterplot of actual versus predicted values.
  • Analyzed the normal distribution of residuals in the histogram.
  • Uncovered strong correlations in the correlation matrix and heatmap.

Result:

The multiple linear regression model produced impressive results, highlighting the importance of mean_distance in predicting fare amounts. Business recommendations included adjusting fare rates based on distance, leveraging mean_duration for accurate time estimates, and exploring rush_hour impacts on fares.


Conclusion:
This project showcased the effectiveness of EDA and multiple linear regression in predicting taxi fares based on historical data. Valuable insights were obtained for the NYC TLC, informing data-driven decision-making, fare pricing strategies, and overall taxi service enhancements. Regression analysis emerged as an essential tool for simplifying complex data relationships and unlocking predictive capabilities across diverse industries.
