Customer Demographics Analysis

  • Category: Data Analysis
  • Tech Stack: Excel, Tableau

    • This is the project that I completed for the virtual internship program of KPMG AU on Forage.


Situation:

The simulation at KPMG AU focused on advising a client on customer targeting, plunging me into the role of a Data Analyst. This provided a hands-on opportunity to apply analytics in a practical setting.

Task:

Integral tasks involved assessing data quality and completeness, followed by an in-depth analysis to target high-value customers based on demographics and attributes. Subsequently, I crafted dashboards to effectively communicate these findings visually.

  • Key tasks included:
    1. Data quality and completeness assessment
    2. Analysis targeting high-value customers
    3. Development of visually engaging dashboards
