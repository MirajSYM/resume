    • Buyer demographics

Action:

To meet program objectives, I meticulously analyzed and visualized the data, crafting an insightful dashboard that simplified complex information.

  • Key actions included:
    • In-depth data analysis

Result:

The outcome was a comprehensive and visually appealing dashboard that not only met program requirements but also illuminated key insights.

  • Notable insights comprised:
    • Concentration in the 35 to 44 age range for sales strategy
    • Varied trends in first-time and repeat buyers across age groups
    • Opportunities for targeted financial solutions and community engagement in minority-prone areas
    • A holistic view of economic landscapes through loan-to-value ratios and median family incomes
