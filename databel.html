Customer Churn Analysis of Databel

  • Category: Data Analysis
  • Tech Stack: Excel, Tableau


Project Brief

The churn rate is a critical business metric that measures the loss of subscribers or employees over a specific time period. It applies to both subscription-based businesses and workforce management. In essence, it represents the opposite of the growth rate, as it quantifies customer or talent attrition. To thrive, a company must consistently acquire more subscriptions or employees than it loses. Industry-specific benchmarks for churn rates provide a valuable competitive perspective.

Task:

My goal was to understand the reasons behind customer churn and identify strategies to reduce it. The dashboard's main concern is to answer all the business questions that will fulfill the customer's need. It provides insights into critical aspects of Databel's operations, helping the company make informed decisions. Some of the questions it answers include:

Business Questions from Stakeholders

  • • What's the total churn rate for "Databel"?
  • • Does the count of unique customers match the count of customers?
  • • Which of the following is not part of the top 5 churn reasons?
  • • What's the most prevalent churn reason?
  • • Which state has the highest churn rate?
  • • Did you discover the churn rate for senior citizens is significantly above the average? What's the churn rate for that group?
  • • What's the average churn rate for people aged between 55 and 60?
  • • Which group size has the lowest churn rate?
  • • For most people who are not part of a group, the churn rate for people in the group is?
  • • What's the churn rate for people on an unlimited plan who consume less than 5 GB of data?
  • • The churn rate of customers who have an international plan but don't call internationally is sky-high. How many customers are part of this group?
  • • What's the average monthly charge for that same group?
  • • How many customers that churned are on a Month-to-Month contract and pay by Direct Debit?
  • • What's the churn rate in Alaska (AK) for customers on a Two-Year contract? (Alaska can be found on the top left of the map.)
  • • What's the churn rate (in %) for customers outside a group who have an account length of 12 months or less?
  • • What's the average number of customer service calls for customers who are on a month-to-month contract and by direct debit?
  • • What's the average extra data charge (two decimal points) for customers who are not on an unlimited data plan and consumed less than 5 gigabytes?

The Dashboard details:

  • "The Overview" Page: Designed and implemented a dynamic dashboard featuring two interactive donut pie charts for slicing and filtering data, a versatile bar plot, and an informative map, collectively providing a comprehensive project overview.
  • "Age Brackets and Groups" Page: Constructed an analytical dashboard presenting detailed insights on age groups and demographics through interactive bar plots, enhanced by user-friendly filtering options for a deeper understanding.
  • "Payment and Contract Type" Dashboard: Engineered an insightful dashboard showcasing diverse payment methods and contract types, enriched with interactive filters to facilitate in-depth exploration and analysis.
  • "Data and International Plan" Dashboard: Conceptualized and executed a multi-faceted dashboard utilizing matrix charts and informative cards to effectively communicate insights, complemented by a dynamic filter for seamless data exploration.
